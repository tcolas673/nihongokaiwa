Grammar 文法

Build up your knowledge on Japanese grammar. This can really speed up how quickly you learn the language. Do not let the Kanji scare you! You should focus on learning on how to construct sentences and little by little you'll learn to associate certain kanji with those words

Sometimes you will get bored of just studying grammar all day. However, don't get discouraged. You can do anything you put your mind to!

This section primarily covers all the parts of speech: nouns, adjectives, verbs, and adverbs. It will also describe how to integrate the various parts of speech into a coherent sentence by using particles. By the end of this section, you should have an understanding of how basic sentences are constructed.

3 Useful resources for Grammar

  1. Jpanese Ammo

    A native Japanese speaker who posts videos on Youtube about how to speak Japanese naturally! This is one of the best free sources for learning new tid bits of Japanese.

  2. Yes Japan

    Lesson by an American who learned Japanese, his lessons cover Japanese grammar and culture. You can watch his videos on Youtube.

  3. Learn Japanese Adventure

    This is a website is very useful and strongly recommended for people who want to strengthen their understanding of Japanese Grammar. It’s basically an online textbook, that’s better than your textbook.
