ようこそ

Welcome to Nihongo Kaiwa! Well, we all know that Japanese may seem a little scary, but this website was made to ease your fears! There are insightful lessons here that run from beginner level to advanced. You'll learn to speak Japanese in no time for free! In fact all that we ask is for you to stay motivated and eager to learn! Let's begin!

Purpose(目的)

The purpose of these lessons is to help you communicate in daily life situations and learn 100 basic Kanji. This course will also help you prepare for the JPLT N5. By the time you finish this course, you'll not only have a good basic vocabulary but you'll have the grammatical tools to start putting your own sentences together and getting some basic conversation happening.

Speak Japanese(日本語話せ)

One of the most important ways you can improve your Japanese is by finding a partner that can speak to you in Japanese! How do you find a partner? Well that's easy! Come to the meeting held on the second floor of the CU building at FAU on Tuesdays and Wednesdays! Please check out our contact us page if you'd like more information!

単語

Build up your knowledge on Japanese words and vocabulary. This can really speed up your Japanese learning process. I can't stress more on the importance of doing the above. Sometimes you will get bored of studying grammar alone. While I don't deny the importance of grammar, increasing your "word inventory" is equally important as well.

漢字
