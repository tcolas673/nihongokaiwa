自己紹介

We are a small on campus club at FAU that provides students an opportunity to practice speaking Japanese. We hold conversations in Japanese and review Japanese grammar with native speakers of the language. We hold meetings every Tuesday and Wednesday at the CU building on campus.

Meeting Times

Meetings are held from 1:30 to 2:30 on Tuesdays and 12:00 to 1:00 on Wednesdays on the second floor of the CU building.
